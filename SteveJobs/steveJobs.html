Interesting Facts

Steven Paul Jobs Was Born on 24 February 1955, and was legally adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs just a few weeks later. His biological sister is the American novelist Mona Simpson.

The first thing Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak sold for profit was a digital Blue Box. It was a device that let you place long distance calls for free. They made around 100 and sold all of them.

Steve Jobs went to Reed Liberal Arts College in Portland Oregon, but he dropped out after 6 months.

The name apple was chosen after Steve Jobs had come home from an apple farm. They decided that if they couldn't come up with a better name within a day they would stick with it. So Apple Computers became the name of their company.

In 1986 Jobs bought the computer division of George Lucas' ILM for $10 million and rebranded it as Pixar

In 1985 Steve Jobs, along with five other "refugees" from Apple resign and start a new computer company called NeXT. He later returned to Apple 1997 and has to rebuild their damaged brand image.
